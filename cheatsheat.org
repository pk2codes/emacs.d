* Cheat sheet 
** Bookmarks
*** C-x r m set bookmark 
*** C-x r l list bookmarks
*** M-x bookmark-delete
** Code Navigation
*** C-q expand region
*** C-M-<mouse-1> add cursor 
*** C-f find file in project
*** C-a ag root 
*** C-c C-c eslint fix all
** Git
*** C-x g status 
** emacs 
*** M-x customize-variable RET check variable 
*** C-h k describe key (for keybinding)
